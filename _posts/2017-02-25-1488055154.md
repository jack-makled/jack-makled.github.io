## 1488055154

---
\\
Here at your side\\
They want to love you\\
A remnant of the world today\\
just to be poetic\\
It can be great\\
Than you have lain,\\
Especially about what we said

looking at your side\\
Drown me in chains\\
Just one more day,\\
And I am now\\
Every piece of content.\\
to all the same?\\
You were never hers.
